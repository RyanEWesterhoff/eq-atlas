⚡ Planes of Power

The Planes of Power represent the final frontier of mortal ambition — divine realms accessible only to the most powerful adventurers, where the landscape itself is shaped by god-level forces and where the creatures that call these places home are not measured in levels but in epochs of accumulated power. Access to the deeper planes requires demonstrated mastery of the earlier ones.
🗺

Progression Path

The planes are not freely accessible — passage to deeper planes requires proof of capability earned in earlier ones. The general progression follows the tiers below. Those without the required flags of passage will find the transit portals sealed to them.

📚 Tier 1 — Entry Planes

📚

Plane of Knowledge

The great nexus — home to the Library of Myrist and the crossroads of all planar travel. See the full zone entry for complete details. This is the hub through which all other planes are accessed. Violence is impossible within its bounds.

Key Features: All guild trainers, all teleportation portals, the Library of Myrist, Zebuxoruk's imprisonment, merchants for every need.

☮

Plane of Tranquility

A realm of absolute peace where the energies of the universe achieve momentary balance. The Plane of Tranquility serves as a staging area and meeting point for adventurers preparing for deeper planar incursions. Like the Plane of Knowledge, violence is not possible within its serene boundaries.

The plane is populated by beings called the Undead — entities of pure serenity who have transcended the mortal desire for conflict. They serve as guides and advisors to those preparing for the deeper planes. The Marble Palace at the plane's center contains the planar transits to Tier 2 destinations.

Key Features: Safe haven, additional merchants, quest-givers for planar progression, gate to all Tier 2 planes.

🛡 Tier 3 — Advanced Planes

🛡

Plane of Valor

Mithaniel Marr's realm — a landscape of eternal battle between warriors of supreme excellence. The Plane of Valor does not distinguish between living and dead, only between those who fight with honor and those who do not. It is simultaneously beautiful and terrible: pristine golden halls where the sounds of combat never cease.

The plane's guardians are the finest warriors ever to fall in honorable battle — they have been elevated by Mithaniel Marr himself to serve as eternal defenders of the realm. They are dangerous not from hatred but from absolute martial competence. Those who fall here are judged instantly — the worthy are acknowledged; the unworthy are dismissed.

⚔ 60–66PaladinsUndead WarriorsElite
⛈

Bastion of Thunder

The fortress of the storm giants at the heart of Karana's domain — a massive keep built from solidified lightning where the four winds converge. The giants here are not the primitive Kromrif of Velious but elemental storm giants of supreme power, each capable of calling down lightning with a gesture.

The Bastion is the gateway to the Plane of Air — only those who have overcome its defenders can access the transit that leads to Xegony's realm. The Hammer of the Storm, a relic of divine meteorological power, is said to be held by the Bastion's lord.

⚔ 62–68Storm GiantsLightningElite

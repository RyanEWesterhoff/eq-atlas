Welcome to Norrath

A World of Adventure, Peril, and Ancient Mystery

Herein lies the collected knowledge of the great Library of Myrist — every land, every danger, every creature that walks, crawls, flies, or haunts the world of Norrath and the planes beyond. From the rolling hills of Antonica to the frozen wastes of Velious, from the jungles of Kunark to the lunar surface of Luclin, and finally to the divine realms of the Planes of Power — this atlas charts the known world.

Each entry preserves an air of mystery to the world. You will find no loot tables or walkthrough material here — only the knowledge a seasoned traveler would share with a trusted companion: where the dangers lie, what power can be found, and the history that shaped every ruin and city.

⚔️ Notable Figures

The rulers, heroes, and villains whose decisions shape the fate of Norrath. These are the figures the party is most likely to encounter, ally with, or stand against across the course of the campaign.

🤝

Allied Leaders

Rulers, champions, and figures who stand alongside the forces of Good — potential patrons, quest-givers, or powerful allies the party may seek aid from during the campaign.

King Tearis Thex
King of the Koada'Dal; Ruler of Felwithe
High Elf • Felwithe, Greater Faydark
A centuries-old sovereign who has ruled Felwithe with unwavering conviction and moral clarity. Father of Firiona Vie, he is known for his uncompromising judgment — "I never forget and I rarely forgive." He commands the Arcane Keep personally and appears only at moments of great consequence.
Firiona Vie
Champion of Tunare; Princess of Felwithe
High Elf • Felwithe / Firiona Vie Outpost, Kunark
Daughter of King Tearis Thex and a paladin blessed personally by the goddess Tunare, Firiona Vie is the most celebrated hero of Norrath's modern age. She led the campaign against the Ring of Scale in Kunark, clashed with Lanys T'Vyl across multiple fronts, and serves as a living symbol of Good's resistance to the encroaching dark.
Antonius Bayle IV
High Keeper of the Sword; Ruler of Qeynos
Human • Qeynos, Antonica
A devout Paladin of Karana who assumed rule of Qeynos at age twenty-four and earned the fierce loyalty of his citizens through just and firm governance. His brother Kane works perpetually against him from within the shadows. Bayle represents the most powerful force for order and Good on the continent of Antonica.
El'Arad
Lord Primarch of Erudin; High Arcane Authority
Erudite • Erudin, Odus
The preeminent political and magical authority of Erudin, the city of the Erudite scholars. El'Arad presides over the High Council and the Academy of the Arcane Sciences, balancing centuries of arcane tradition against the constant threat of the heretic Paineel faction. A figure of immense intellect and guarded ambition.
Mayor Gubbin
Mayor of Rivervale
Halfling • Rivervale, Antonica
The wealthiest halfling ever to live, having made his fortune adventuring alongside Antonius Bayle III and securing a great treasure hoard from the fairiefolk of Lesser Faydark. A former rogue turned civic leader, he now governs Rivervale quietly — keeping his storied past deliberately out of conversation.
King Kazon Stormhammer
King of Kaladim; Lord of the Dwarves
Dwarf • South Kaladim, Faydwer
A formidable warrior-king who rules the Dwarven stronghold of Kaladim from his throne room deep within the mountain fortress. Protected by the Bloodforge Brigade — his most elite honor guard — Stormhammer embodies the stubborn martial pride of the Dwarven people and their long alliance with the forces of Good.
King Ak'Anon
Immortal Clockwork Sovereign of the Gnomes
Clockwork • Ak'Anon, Steamfont Mountains
The greatest invention of all gnomekind — a mechanical ruler infused with the memories and accumulated wisdom of the original King Horatio Ak'Anon, reborn in clockwork so that gnomish civilization might benefit from his guidance in perpetuity. He has presided over Ak'Anon for nearly five centuries, a marvel of engineering and governance both.
Halas Council of Elders
Governing Body of the Northmen Nation
Barbarian • Halas, Everfrost Peaks
Fourteen Elders chosen by merit from fourteen scattered Northmen tribes, the Council governs through decentralization by design — a system devised by the reformer Burke Wingfoot after the fall of the ancient Barbarian Kingdom to ensure no single ruler could again corrupt the nation. They communicate through scouts and meet at hidden, rotating locations.
Al'Kabor
The Most Powerful Wizard in All of Norrath
Erudite • West Freeport / Dreadlands / Luclin
An arrogant and brilliant Erudite wizard who has devoted centuries to research on the Combine Empire, the Kedge race, and arcane immortality. He traveled with Firiona Vie's company and personally confronted Phinigel Autropos in the depths of Kedge Keep. His obsessive research eventually opened a portal to Luclin — triggering storms that reshaped the age.
Tsaph Katta
Imperator of the Combine Empire
Human • Katta Castellum, Luclin
The visionary founder-emperor who sought to unite all races of Norrath — including the dark ones — under a single banner of civilization. Betrayed and poisoned by his own general Seru at the First Combine Summit, legend says he was sealed in magical stasis by loyal followers who fled to the moon Luclin. He survives as a comatose symbol of a lost age of unity, preserved in the city built in his name.

Enemies & Threats

Warlords, tyrants, and ancient evils who oppose the party and the forces of Good across Norrath. These figures command armies, cults, and dark powers that shape the campaign's greatest conflicts.

Mayong Mistmoore
Lord of Castle Mistmoore; First Vampire of Norrath
Ancient Elf (Cursed) • Castle Mistmoore, Lesser Faydark
One of the oldest living creatures on Norrath — an elf of a forgotten age cursed to become the very first vampire. Vain, sophisticated, and calculating, he has spent millennia accumulating arcane knowledge, creating the drachnid race, and maintaining a vast spy network across Norrath. His ultimate goal is godhood — a goal he has been methodically working toward for centuries.
Lord Inquisitor Seru
Lord Inquisitor of the Combine Inquisition; Betrayer of the Empire
Human (Transformed) • Sanctus Seru, Luclin
Once Tsaph Katta's most trusted general, Seru's fanatical belief in racial purity drove him to poison his own emperor. It is said that he chased Katta to Luclin with the Combine Inquisition becoming trapped there after his hasty decision.
Jurglash
Warlord; Founder of the Grobb Clan
Troll • Innothule Swamp / Grobb, Antonica
The troll warlord who rose from the ruins of the Ykeshan Clan and unified the scattered trolls of the Innothule Swamp into the Grobb Clan. Under his leadership, shamanic traditions devoted to Cazic-Thule and Innoruuk were established, the Nightkeep was built, and Da Bashers were forged into a feared fighting force. His legacy endures in every troll who calls Grobb home.
Naythox Thex
King of Neriak; Lord of the Teir'Dal
Dark Elf • Darklight Palace, Neriak, Nektulos Forest
A young but formidable dark elf warrior-king, great-grandchild of Nevar Thex the founder of the House of Thex. He rules Neriak from Darklight Palace through the Indigo Brotherhood (his warrior elite) and the Dreadguard. His politically turbulent marriage to Queen Cristanos — arranged to unify the four arcane circles of dark elf power — has devolved into bitter civil war.
Emperor Vekin
First Emperor of the Cabilisian Empire; Founder of Modern Iksar Power
Iksar • Vekin Palace, Cabilis East, Kunark
The warlord who rebuilt Iksar civilization from ruin, uniting the surviving clans through martial supremacy and founding the Cabilisian Empire with Cabilis as its capital. Descended from the Army of Ik's elite soldiers, he was notorious for his love of torture and maintained vast dungeons beneath his palace. He viewed the resurrected Venril Sathir as the gravest threat to his rising empire.
Sir Lucan D'Lere
Commander of the Freeport Militia
Human • West Freeport, Antonica
A former Paladin of the Knights of Truth who fell from grace after killing a fellow knight in a dispute, then formed the Freeport Militia — a corrupt paramilitary force staffed by mercenaries and criminals. He rules West Freeport through intimidation and bribery, and is a persistent nemesis to good-aligned adventurers passing through the continent's most dangerous city.
Overlord Virate Manaar
Overlord of Paineel; Protector of the Seekers
Erudite • Paineel (The Hole), Odus
The military commander of Darkglow Palace and protector of Paineel's necromantic Seekers of the Dark Truth. Virate Manaar represents the martial arm of the Heretics — Erudite outcasts who worship Cazic-Thule and practice shadow knight arts and necromancy. He ensures the Seekers can conduct their dark research undisturbed by rivals or outside forces.
Lanys T'Vyl
Daughter of Innoruuk; Champion of Hate
Dark Elf • Plane of Hate / Norrath
Created directly from Innoruuk's own essence and gifted with his boundless hate, Lanys T'Vyl is the god's living weapon — the most powerful dark elf ever born. She has clashed with Firiona Vie across multiple campaigns, serving as her dark mirror in an ongoing struggle between divine hate and divine love. Brilliant, ruthless, and utterly devoted to her father's purpose.
